📢 What is BookTab?

BookTab is a lightweight and elegant bookmark beautification extension for Google Chrome and Edge browsers. It helps transform your browser bookmarks into a streamlined browser navigation homepage, making it easier and quicker to access your bookmarks in the form of a bookmark tree.

✨ What are its Highlights?

☀️ It is Beautiful and Lightweight

  • It follows the “less is more” design philosophy. The page design remains simple and restrained.

🔖 Unleash your Bookmarks Bar

  • Transform your bookmarks page into a new homepage .
  • Expand your bookmarks in a tree view .
  • Freely rearrange your bookmarks .
  • Set your frequently used bookmarks as quick links .
  • Multiple search engine switching without repeated input.

🚀 Sidebar shortcuts

  • You can quickly access search engines and your favorite websites via the sidebar on any site.
  • support searching copied text immediately after copying.
  • no need to re-enter, simply switch between search engines to search for the same term.

📢 什么是 BookTab？

BookTab 是一个轻量且优雅的书签美化扩展，适用于 Google Chrome 和 Edge 浏览器。它能将浏览器的书签转变为简洁的浏览导航主页，使你能够以书签树的形式更轻松快捷地访问书签。

✨ 它有哪些亮点？

☀️ 美观且轻量

  • 遵循“少即是多”的设计理念。页面设计简洁克制。

🔖 释放你的书签栏

  • 将你的书签页转换为新标签页。
  • 以树形状态展开你的书签。
  • 自由地重新排列你的书签。
  • 设置常用书签为快速链接。
  • 支持多个搜索引擎切换，无需重复输入。

🚀 侧边栏快捷方式

  • 你可以在任何网站通过侧边栏快速访问搜索引擎和你喜欢的网站。
  • 支持复制文本后即可搜索复制的文本信息。
  • 无需重复输入，同一搜索词在不同搜索引擎间切换即可搜索。
